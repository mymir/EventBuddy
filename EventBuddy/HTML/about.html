Our Mission

EventBuddy's mission is to connect college students with their peers in a safe, effective way. This app helps students keep track of what events are going on, find people to go to events with, and make friends with other students on campus. EventBuddy gives students the tools they need to participate more on campus and to fully take advantage of everything their university has to offer.

v

How To Use This App

After a university has signed up for an EventBuddy account, anyone width a student email adress can create a profile.

Once a profile has been created, the student can view and filter through different events going on around campus on the Events page.

There they can either join the event, or add the event to their Interested list. This list lets other students know that this student is looking for someone to go to the event with.

Students can even utalize the Maps page to see where events are taking place. From there they can click on markers to get more information about the event.
